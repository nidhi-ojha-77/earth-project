Few facts about the world we live in

Earth, our home planet, is the third planet from the Sun and the only known place in the universe with life. It is a rocky, fifth-largest planet in the solar system, distinguished by its abundance of liquid water covering about 71% of its surface, an oxygen-rich atmosphere, and active plate tectonics. Earth's axis is tilted, causing seasons, and it is orbited by one moon, which stabilizes its climate and affects ocean tides.
